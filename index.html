Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

For the Gulab jamun Balls:-


  • 1 cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • ¼ cup milk (warm, adjust as needed)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional, helps in soft texture)
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt (optional, for extra softness)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying

For the Sugar Syrup(Chashni):-


  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon rose water (optional, for fragrance)
  • Few strands of saffron (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (to prevent crystallization)

Recipe

Step 1: Prepare the Sugar Syrup


  1. In a pan, add sugar and water and heat it on medium flame.
  2. Stir until the sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Add cardamom pods, saffron strands, and lemon juice (to prevent crystallization).
  4. Let the syrup simmer for 4-5 minutes until it becomes slightly sticky (no string consistency needed).
  5. Turn off the heat and add rose water. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Gulab Jamun Dough


  1. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add ghee and mix well using your fingers until the mixture looks crumbly.
  3. Gradually add warm milk, lemon juice, and yogurt while kneading gently into a soft, smooth dough.
  4. Do not over-knead; just mix until everything comes together.
  5. Cover the dough and let it rest for 10 minutes.

Step 3: Shape the Jamuns


  1. Divide the dough into small equal portions.
  2. Roll them into smooth balls (make sure there are no cracks).
  3. Grease your hands with ghee to avoid sticking.

Step 4: Fry the Gulab Jamun


  1. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan on low-medium heat.
  2. Drop a small piece of dough into the oil; if it slowly rises, the temperature is perfect.
  3. Gently add the Gulab Jamun balls into the oil, stirring constantly to cook evenly.
  4. Fry until they turn golden brown (takes about 5-7 minutes).
  5. Remove them from the oil and drain on paper towels.

Step 5: Soak in Sugar Syrup


  1. Add the warm Gulab Jamun into the hot (not boiling) sugar syrup.
  2. Let them soak for at least 1-2 hours to absorb the syrup fully.

Serve & Enjoy!
